Frequently asked questions

Is there a limit to how many friends I can refer?

No limit at all! You can refer as many friends as you want. Each successful referral that makes a purchase earns you a 10% commission.

What counts as a valid referral?

A valid referral is someone who (1) signs up using your unique referral link or code, and (2) completes a purchase of any Tungo package. Sign-ups alone without a purchase don't generate a commission.

Can I refer someone who already has an account?

The referral system tracks new sign-ups. If your friend already has a Tungo account, they won't count as a new referral. Share your link with people who haven't signed up yet.
